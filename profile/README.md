Getting Started with Bloqr
Quick tip
No technical knowledge required — Bloqr does the heavy lifting for you.
Get set up in 3 simple steps
Create your account at app.bloqr.dev.
Choose your filter lists (what you want to block).
Copy your personal DNS address or download your compiled list and add it to your device or router.
What Bloqr helps block
Block Ads
Stops ad networks so pages load cleaner and with fewer interruptions.
Block Trackers
Reduces hidden tracking tools that follow your activity across websites.
Block Malware
Helps prevent known harmful domains from loading on your network.
